  • Personal Data means any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual.

2.2 Non-Personal Data

3.2 User Choices

You can manage cookie settings in your browser and opt out of certain cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our Services. To opt out of Google Analytics, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data, including but not limited to:

10. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

If you reside in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California (USA), you have specific rights under GDPR or CCPA, including:

  • Right to Access: Request information on what personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.
  • Right to Rectification: Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Right to Erasure (Deletion): Request the deletion of your personal data where no valid legal reason for its retention exists.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: In certain circumstances, request that we limit how your data is processed.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other legitimate interests.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination (CCPA): We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@growthworld.net. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.
